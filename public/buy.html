Financing prep — what you actually need

Hometown Heroes DPA: up to $35,000 toward down payment + closing for eligible Florida first-time buyers (veterans exempt from first-time rule). Min credit 640. Income up to 150% county AMI.

D.R. Horton Flex Cash: 6%+ in builder credits stackable with low-rate buy-downs on new construction. Move-in ready homes with builder warranty.

Self-employed? Bank-statement and 1099 loan products available. We work with lenders who specialize in non-W-2 borrowers — don't let a tax-return myth stop you.

FHA / VA / Conventional: 0% to 5% down depending on the program. We'll model true monthly cost — including Florida insurance and property tax — before you write an offer.
