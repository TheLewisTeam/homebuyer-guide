Your Polk County Home Buyer's Guide

The exact 6-step path our clients take from pre-approval to keys — plus the 5 mistakes first-time buyers make in Central Florida and how to skip them.

The 6-step path to your keys

Step 1

Get Pre-Approved

⏱ 1–2 weeks

Know what you can borrow before you fall in love with a house.

  • Lender reviews your income, credit, and debts
  • You get a pre-approval letter showing your max loan amount
  • Sellers take your offers seriously once you have this letter
Insider tip: Self-employed? We work with lenders who specialize in bank-statement and 1099 loans. Don't let a W-2 myth stop you.
Step 2

Define Wants vs. Needs

⏱ 1 week

Separate must-haves from nice-to-haves before you tour.

  • We walk through your lifestyle, commute, schools, future plans
  • You rank what matters most — bedrooms, yard, location, style
  • We set up a custom search so you only see homes that fit
Insider tip: Buyers who write it down before touring close 2x faster than buyers who "know it when they see it."
Step 3

House Hunting

⏱ 2–8 weeks

Tour smart. Compare honestly. Trust the process.

  • Scheduled showings with us — you never tour alone
  • Private notes and a comparison sheet for every home
  • Market intel on neighborhoods, trends, and time-on-market
Insider tip: The house that "checks every box" is rare. The house that checks the RIGHT boxes is the one.
Step 4

Make an Offer

⏱ 1–3 days

Strategy matters more than price.

  • We analyze comps and market conditions together
  • We draft the offer: price, earnest money, contingencies, timeline
  • Negotiation — counters, multiple offers, tradeoffs
Insider tip: Marry the house, date the rate. Today's prices won't wait — you can always refinance later.
Step 5

Inspection & Appraisal

⏱ 2–3 weeks

Under contract. Due diligence begins.

  • Home inspection — you hire a licensed inspector
  • Appraisal — lender hires one to verify value
  • Negotiate repairs or credits based on findings
Insider tip: Cosmetic issues are for you to fix. Structural, roof, HVAC, plumbing — those are negotiation items.
Step 6

Closing Day

⏱ 1 day

Sign the papers. Get the keys. Welcome home.

  • Final walkthrough the day of closing
  • Sign loan documents at title company
  • Wire funds for closing costs and down payment
  • Keys handed over — the home is yours
Insider tip: Do NOT make any large purchases or open new credit between pre-approval and closing. It can kill your loan.

The 5 mistakes first-time Polk County buyers make

1

Skipping pre-approval (or settling for "pre-qualified")

Pre-qualification is a phone-call estimate. Pre-approval is a documented underwriting decision. Sellers ignore offers without pre-approval letters. Every single day matters in this market.

2

Ignoring Florida insurance + tax in the monthly budget

Florida insurance has shifted dramatically. Your "monthly payment" without insurance + property tax is fiction. We'll model the true monthly cost before you write an offer.

3

Not knowing about Hometown Heroes DPA + builder Flex Cash

Florida has up to $35,000 in down-payment assistance for first-time buyers. New-construction builders are stacking 6%+ Flex Cash on top. Most buyers leave $20K-$50K on the table because no one told them.

4

Buying outside the lake / Legoland tourism corridor without checking STR rules

Some Polk County neighborhoods allow short-term rentals. Some don't. The wrong purchase costs you the rental income you were counting on. Always verify HOA + county STR rules before writing.

5

Making a big purchase between pre-approval and closing

Any new credit (car, furniture, credit card) between pre-approval and closing can kill your loan at the finish line. Lenders re-pull your credit days before closing. Wait until after the keys are in your hand.
